About us

Bensaddik Mohamed,

Founder
The E-tude project was born out of a personal journey in education, where every step illuminated the need for a more dynamic and customizable digital learning experience. Growing up surrounded by diverse educational systems and later navigating the complexities of higher education, I often found myself pondering the gaps and inefficiencies in traditional learning platforms. The idea took shape gradually, evolving through insights gained from personal experiences, discussions with peers, and observations of evolving educational technologies.

This project isn't merely a requirement but a culmination of passion and dedication to crafting a solution that empowers educators and learners alike. It's a testament to the belief that education should be adaptive, accessible, and engaging for every learner, regardless of their background or learning style.

The journey of developing E-tude has been a transformative one, from conceptualization to execution, each phase enriched by the feedback and collaboration of mentors and peers. This project stands as a Portfolio Project for Holberton School, where I've had the privilege to delve deeper into the intricacies of software development and user-centered design. It's an invitation to explore how innovation in education can reshape the future of learning.
